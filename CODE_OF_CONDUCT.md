# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

FoodSnapAI is committed to creating an open, respectful, and inclusive environment for everyone.  
We want this project to be a safe space for learning, collaboration, and sharing ideas about food, cooking, and technology without discrimination or harassment.

We welcome participation from all people regardless of age, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, race, ethnicity, nationality, religion, level of experience, or background.

## Expected Behavior

All contributors, users, and community members are expected to:

- be respectful and polite in communication;
- give constructive feedback;
- be open to different ideas and perspectives;
- support a friendly and collaborative environment;
- respect other people’s work, time, and effort;
- use inclusive and professional language.

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behavior is not acceptable in the FoodSnapAI community:

- harassment, bullying, or personal attacks;
- discriminatory, offensive, or insulting comments;
- hateful speech or exclusionary language;
- trolling, hostile arguments, or repeated disruptive behavior;
- sharing private information about others without permission;
- posting harmful, abusive, or inappropriate content.

## Project Values

FoodSnapAI supports the principles of non-discrimination and do no harm.  
The project is intended to encourage positive social interaction, recipe discovery, and culinary learning in a way that is safe and respectful for all users. This aligns with the project’s goal of building a positive and inclusive platform around food and technology.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- the GitHub repository;
- issues, pull requests, and discussions;
- project documentation;
- any community spaces related to FoodSnapAI.

## Reporting Problems

If you experience or witness inappropriate behavior, please report it to the project maintainer.

Contact: **maksimsarsekeev@gmail.com**

All reports will be reviewed respectfully and handled as fairly and confidentially as possible.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct.  
They may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, or other contributions that violate these rules.

In serious cases, maintainers may temporarily or permanently restrict participation in the project.

## Final Note

By participating in FoodSnapAI, you agree to follow this Code of Conduct and help maintain a welcoming and respectful community for everyone.
